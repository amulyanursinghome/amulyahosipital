About Amulya Nursing Home

Amulya Nursing Home was started on 8th May 1992 at Narasaraopet. It is fully equipped with Two Operation Theatres and 'C' arm. ICU facility available for Trauma cases, Fully equipped Laboratary and Pharmacy Round the Clock. Amulya Nursing Home was exclusively an ORTHOPEDIC Specialisation hospital, giving comprehensive care to the patients of Guntur, Prakasam and Nalgonda Districts.

AIMS : To do service for the Trauma & Emergency Services and to Eradicate Poliomyelitis.

GOALS : Eradicate Poliomyelitis.

VISION : Detect, Prevent and Educate about Osteoarthritis.
